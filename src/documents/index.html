Livestream

Watch this years Extinction Marathon: Visions of the Future broadcast directly from the Serpentine Galleries in London, transmitting on-stage contributions over two days including artists, writers, scientists, film-makers, choreographers, theorists and musicians exploring the complex topic of extinction.

Extinction Sites

Extinction Sites plots via submission locations of geographical and historical significance to EXTINCTION - cataloging the psychogeography of the anthropocene through species loss, natural resource depletion and the collapse of civilisation.

Case Studies

The Case Studies Index charts the efforts of organisations and networks to respond to ongoing and emergent crises. Expanding over time, initiatives are filed under the headings FINITE, HUMANITARIAN, DESPAIR, INFRASTRUCTURE and FINANCE.

Texts

In response to the topic of #EXTINCTION, five short essays have been co-commissioned by the Serpentine and The SPACE. Including Benjamin Bratton, Keller Easterling, Maurizio Lazzarato, and Timothy Morton.

EXTINCT.LY

EXTINCTION

EXTINCT.LY is an ongoing commissioning and research platform developed to support remote participation in the Serpentine Galleries’ 2014 Extinction Marathon: Visions of the Future. Designed on the principle that ongoing engagement, collective research and collaboration are required to face and understand an uncertain future, EXTINCT.LY aims to capture the energy of the two day Extinction Marathon as a catalyst for sustained investigation into an inescapable and critical subject of importance to all life on earth.

The live blog and live stream accompany special commissions by artists, writers, speculative designers and programmers, as well as research and data relating to the theme of Extinction. Running in parallel to the two-day event, the platform is intended to unfold and expand over time. Over the following months, EXTINCT.LY will begin to grow into a globally available resource, focused on the continued development of artist-led projects, data visualization, and a combination of online and offline encounters.
